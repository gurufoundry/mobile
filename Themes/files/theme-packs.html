Recommendation

Ship 2–3 packs at MVP launch. Don't overload the gallery.

The full 8 packs are a roadmap, not a launch plan. For MVP I'd ship the original 12 generic presets plus Bonbon Drop (kawaii, broadly appealing across age groups), Bloom Lines (Mother's Day timing, May 1–14), and one of Y2K Chrome or Cottage Forager based on which audience you're testing first.

Treat the rest as post-launch drops — release one new pack every 2–3 weeks. This gives users a reason to come back, lets you A/B which themes get the most stickers created, and means each release feels intentional rather than diluted by choice overload.

Why these packs and not others Each pack is grounded in at least 2 trend sources from the 2026 research pass. Skipped: aesthetics that are oversaturated (clean-girl minimalism, dark academia in its first wave), too narrow (e.g., Frutiger Aero — beautiful but a corporate aesthetic), or fading (cottagecore is steady, not rising). Mother's Day-specific packs were limited to 2 because the holiday is one Sunday — too many themed packs would dilute the moment.
