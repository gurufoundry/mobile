The Concept

A printable sticker that holds a voice. Tap it with your phone, the moment plays back.

An AI-guided creator where you chat with an agent, get a custom sticker design in seconds, then attach a voice note. The NFC-enabled sticker becomes a physical bookmark to a memory, message, or moment — scanned to replay the audio inside the app.

03 / DESIGN SYSTEM

A look that earns trust and feels human

2026 sticker culture is leaning into tactile, imperfect, sensory design — away from sterile minimalism. The look pairs a warm paper background with a confident editorial type system and high-energy accent colors. It feels designed, not generated.

Body & UI
Inter Tight

Clean, neutral, and dense enough for chat bubbles, captions, and form fields. Inter Tight gives the interface its calm baseline so the personality lives in the headlines, color, and motion.

+ JetBrains Mono · Labels & meta

Why this pairing: Fraunces is a contemporary serif with optical sizing — gives editorial warmth without feeling old. Inter Tight is grounded and modern. Together they signal "made with care" rather than "made by template."

04 / MVP SCOPE

What ships, what waits

Build for a single user (you) first. Ship a creator that turns a 2-minute conversation into a printable sticker with optional audio, and prove the loop works before adding social, marketplace, or team features.

In MVP

Phase 1 — Web

  • Email + Apple/Google sign-in
  • Curated preset gallery (10–15 templates)
  • Guided AI chat creator (Claude API)
  • Sticker preview with quick edits
  • PNG + print-ready PDF export
  • Audio recording & cloud storage
  • Personal library
  • NFC tag URL routing (scan → playback page)
Phase 2+

Later

  • Native iOS / Android (React Native)
  • Direct NFC writing from device
  • Order-physical-stickers fulfillment
  • Sharing / sticker recipients without account
  • Sticker collections & albums
  • Themed template packs (seasonal)
  • Voice-to-sticker (audio drives design)
  • Public template marketplace
